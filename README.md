# Best Agri Resources

This is a list of all useful agri related websites and resources I have come across

## Government and International Organizations
- [FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations)](http://www.fao.org/)
- [USDA (United States Department of Agriculture)](https://www.usda.gov/)
- [European Commission - Agriculture and Rural Development](https://ec.europa.eu/info/food-farming-fisheries_en)
- [CGIAR (Consultative Group on International Agricultural Research)](https://www.cgiar.org/)

## Databases
- [AllMachines - the farm equipment database](https://www.allmachines.com)

- [AgWeb](https://www.agweb.com/)
